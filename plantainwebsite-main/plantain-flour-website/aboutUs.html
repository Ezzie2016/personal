About

Our journey began with a simple love for plantains. What started as a desire to bring more plantain-based foods to our community has now evolved into a brand that celebrates the richness and versatility of this amazing fruit. At Plantation, we’re dedicated to making plantains a key part of every meal while supporting local farmers and promoting sustainability.
